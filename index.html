Uncommon Makes is a day-long experience focused on encouraging University of Chicago students to engage with new technologies, build a project of their own, and get involved with the tech community on campus. We'll provide space, food, workshops, time, and support for you to have a uniquely uncommon time.

FAQ

  • Where is Uncommon Makes?
    Uncommon Makes it at the Polsky Exchange North, 1452 E 53rd St. It's accessible by the Polsky Express and 53rd St. Express shuttles by day, and the Central and East shuttles by night.
  • I've never been to a hackathon, can I come?
    Of course! This is a space for you to explore on your own terms and learn something new. We will be providing as much support as we can along the way.
  • What should I bring?
    Your laptop and your spirit to learn! All food, drink, and fun will be provided.
  • Do I have to be a CS major to participate?
    Everyone is welcome, regardless of major.
  • I'm a graduate student at UChicago, can I come?
    Yes, definitely!
  • When does registration close?
    Wednesday, November 8th.
  • I don't attend UChicago, can I come?
    Unfortunately not.
  • Do I need a team?
    Nope! But we will facilitate making teams ;)
